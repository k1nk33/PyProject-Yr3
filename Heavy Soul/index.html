Heavy Soul are a band like few others. Their unrelenting self belief and songwriting abilities have earned them praise on an international level , from their hometown of Edenderry in the Irish midlands to major events in Glasgow ,scotland and London , England. They have also gained notoriety with the Irish/American community in the U.S.A., having four of their tracks played on IRISHRADIO.com to wonderful ovation from the presenter and listener's alike.

renound for their live performances, this is one band that you want to see practising their profession.
